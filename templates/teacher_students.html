How to register students

  1. 1 Download the sample CSV template below.
  2. 2 Share the empty CSV with your students in your group chat or by email.
  3. 3 Each student fills in their row: group name, first name, last name, and email address.
  4. 4 Collect the completed CSV and upload it using the form below. The system will create accounts and send each student their login credentials automatically.
Tips:
  • Each student is identified by their email — uploading the same CSV twice is safe, duplicates are skipped.
  • Student accounts receive a login email automatically once an email provider is configured.
  • The group name in the CSV is created automatically if it does not exist yet.
